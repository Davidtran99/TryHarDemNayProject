# Flow Veo3 Prompt - Camera Movement: Cổng Hoa Giấy và Cầu Gỗ

**Ngày tạo:** 2025-11-16  
**Category:** general  
**Mục đích:** Prompt tiếng Anh cho Flow Veo3 để tạo video với camera di chuyển, mô tả vị trí cổng hoa giấy bên trái cầu, đường lát đá, và cổng gỗ song song với cầu

---

## 1. Prompt Chính Cho Flow Veo3

### 1.1. Prompt Chi Tiết (Full Version - Sát Thực Tế)

```
Cinematic tracking shot, smooth camera movement forward along a rustic dirt road at dusk or overcast day. The scene shows:

CAMERA MOVEMENT:
- Smooth dolly forward movement, camera glides along the dirt road
- Low angle view, camera height at 1.2-1.5 meters
- Slow, steady forward motion (walking pace)
- Camera slightly pans left to reveal the bougainvillea arch gate
- Depth of field: foreground slightly blurred, mid-ground and background in sharp focus

FOREGROUND & PATH:
- Wide dirt road (3-4 meters wide) made of light brown to reddish-brown earth
- Road surface shows tire tracks and wheel marks from vehicles
- Road may be muddy or have puddles of water after rain
- Natural, rustic, unpaved road surface
- Road curves gently as camera moves forward

LEFT SIDE (Camera's Left):
- A grand bougainvillea archway gate positioned parallel to the wooden bridge
- The arch gate is densely covered with vibrant pink (60-70%) and white (30-40%) bougainvillea flowers
- Natural brown wooden support posts (not dark wood, but natural brown wood)
- The arch gate creates a floral frame on the left side of the composition
- A winding dirt path leads toward this wooden arch gate, visible when looking left
- Dense dark green foliage, tall bamboo, and trees behind the arch gate
- The arch gate and wooden bridge are positioned parallel to each other

CENTER PATH:
- The main dirt road continues forward
- Road leads directly to the wooden bridge ahead
- The road is the main route connecting the arch gate area to the bridge

RIGHT SIDE (Camera's Right):
- Higher ground with scattered trees and vegetation
- Natural landscape, not manicured
- May have some wildflowers or natural plants

MID-GROUND:
- A small muddy stream (1.5-2.5 meters wide) flowing across the dirt road
- Stream water is brown and murky (not clear)
- May have concrete culvert pipes (some may be broken)
- Natural stream banks with earth and vegetation
- A rustic wooden bridge (3-4 meters long) spanning the stream ahead
- The bridge is made of natural brown wood with simple wooden railings
- The bridge may have pink and white bougainvillea flowers on railings matching the arch gate
- The bridge is positioned parallel to the bougainvillea arch gate (both structures run parallel to each other)
- The dirt road leads from the foreground directly to the bridge

BACKGROUND:
- Tall bamboo groves and dense dark green foliage on both sides
- Natural, rustic landscape
- Overcast gray sky with clouds (not bright sunny day)
- May see distant trees and natural vegetation

LIGHTING:
- Soft diffused natural light from overcast sky
- Natural lighting, not artificial LED strips
- May have warm natural light if at dusk
- Realistic, natural atmosphere

COMPOSITION:
- The bougainvillea arch gate on the left and the wooden bridge ahead are parallel structures
- The dirt road runs between them, connecting the arch gate area to the bridge
- When camera looks left, the winding dirt path leading to the wooden arch gate is visible
- The main dirt road leads forward to the bridge
- Both the arch gate and bridge are visible in the same frame, positioned parallel to each other

STYLE:
- Photorealistic, highly detailed
- Professional cinematic videography
- Natural, rustic, tropical farm aesthetic
- Realistic colors: brown earth, green vegetation, murky brown water
- Smooth camera movement, no shaky footage
- Natural motion blur appropriate for walking pace
- Aspect ratio 16:9
- High resolution, cinematic quality
- Duration: 10-15 seconds
- Tropical style, natural, rustic, authentic farm entrance
```

## 2. Prompt Tập Trung Vào Vị Trí Song Song

### 2.1. Prompt Nhấn Mạnh Vị Trí Song Song

```
Cinematic tracking shot moving forward along a garden path, revealing two parallel structures:

PARALLEL STRUCTURES:
- LEFT: A grand bougainvillea archway gate with pink and white flowers, dark wooden frame
- CENTER-AHEAD: A wooden bridge spanning a stream, with matching pink and white bougainvillea flowers on railings
- Both structures are positioned PARALLEL to each other, running in the same direction
- The arch gate is on the left side of the wooden bridge
- Both are visible in the same frame as camera moves forward

CAMERA MOVEMENT:
- Smooth dolly forward along stone-paved path
- Camera height: 1.2-1.5 meters (low angle)
- Slow, steady forward motion
- Camera slightly pans left to reveal the full arch gate
- Both parallel structures remain in frame throughout the shot

PATH AND ROUTE:
- Main stone path (1.5-2m wide) with rectangular light-colored slabs and green grass between stones
- Path leads forward directly to the wooden bridge
- When camera looks left: winding dirt path visible leading to the bougainvillea arch gate
- The stone path with green grass borders connects the arch gate area to the bridge
- Warm golden LED lighting guides the way

COMPOSITION:
- Arch gate (left) and bridge (center-ahead) are parallel
- Stone path runs between them, connecting both areas
- Stream on left side of main path
- Both structures visible simultaneously, creating balanced composition

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, warm golden lighting at dusk, 16:9, smooth camera movement, 10-15 seconds.
```

As camera moves forward:
- The main stone path with green grass borders leads directly ahead to a wooden bridge spanning a stream

When camera looks LEFT:
- A grand bougainvillea archway gate is visible, positioned parallel to the wooden bridge
- The arch gate is densely covered with vibrant pink (60-70%) and white (30-40%) bougainvillea flowers
- A winding dirt path leads from the main stone path toward this wooden arch gate
- The arch gate and wooden bridge are positioned PARALLEL to each other, both structures running in the same direction
- Dense dark green foliage is behind the arch gate

The stone path with green grass borders is the main route connecting the arch gate area (left) to the bridge (center-ahead). Both the arch gate and bridge are visible in the same frame, creating a balanced composition with parallel structures.

Camera: Low angle (1.2-1.5m), smooth dolly forward, slow steady pace, slightly pans left. Warm golden LED lighting (2700K-3000K), soft diffused twilight sky. Photorealistic, professional cinematic, warm color palette, depth of field, 16:9 aspect ratio, 10-15 seconds duration.

- ✅ Bắt đầu với camera movement description
- ✅ Mô tả vị trí các yếu tố rõ ràng (LEFT, CENTER, RIGHT)
- ✅ Nhấn mạnh mối quan hệ "parallel" giữa arch gate và bridge
- ✅ Mô tả đường dẫn và kết nối giữa các yếu tố

### 6.2. Từ Khóa Quan Trọng
